Asking Good Questions

12/07/2014

One of the most important things to learn as a beginner or even as an expert is how to ask a question. Being able to ask a question correctly can be the difference between getting you question answered and just putting out a question that you hope will get an answer. So here are a couple tips to help get the answers you want.

  • Have a title that summarizes your question

    • The title to you post should never be vague. You need to catch the eyes of the reader and being vague just doesn’t help anyone. When writing your title you want to have a summarize version of your question or problem.

  • Give as much information you can

    • The most important thing you need to do when asking a question is give details. Try to be specific about what your problem could be. Give the error messages and other things to reproduce the issue. This information will make it easier for people to pinpoint a solution.
