Here we are again fellow boot campers and other beginners like myself. It’s the end of week 2 and I want talk briefly about what was cover this week. This week covered developing your own website from the sitemap at the very beginning to the writing your own HTML and CSS. And what comes to mine when I think about HTML and CSS is element selectors.

I know element selectors don’t seem very exciting but they are still important in building a website that can be maintained and updated. The next thing that you need to know is that naming for classes and id should not be abstract. You want be specific I repeat you want to be specific. And when I say specific I don’t mean abbreviations. Abbreviations are a big no no in most cases.
