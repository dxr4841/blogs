Privacy

Privacy is important value to me because I feel there are something that I don’t need to be shared with others. I see privacy as way that you control things about yourself and I feel having that control is what keeps me level headed. I feel that when you have privacy in your life you’re able to control how certain things are shared in your life. I feel for me personally the amount and type of private information I share with someone helps me understand how important that person is in my life. So I use privacy as a way to define many of my relationships.

Values and Stereotype threat

When it comes to my values I feel that are what get me through many of my day to day activities and deal with the stresses that I my feel. When it comes to my values dealing with stereotype threat I do feel that the values I choosing in life helps me deal. Like, one of the threats that young black males deal with is be knew for being dishonest and having not commitment. But, the thing that are important to me are being able to commit and have integrity in all things. So, as long as I keep these values in mind I usually don’t have to deal with many of my stereotype threats. However, there are still going to be time when I feel down and at point I feel that being able to open up to other people will help.
